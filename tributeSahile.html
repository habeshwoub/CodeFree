Ethiopian icon, story teller, artist and beyond all father figure!

Timeline of Tesfaye

  • Place of Birth: Tesfaye was born in 1924 in Oromia State of Bale, in a special place called Kedu to his mother Yewenzwerk Belete and his father Ejersa Bedan.
  • Artist Tesfaye Sahilu: Famous singer and actor. He played different characters in Ethiopian plays including Mekenetwan Tefta, Tela Shach, Netsu Dem, and Tewodros.
  • Children program: Served in ETV (EBC) for 41 years in the children program.
  • The Story teller: Great story teller and narator who shaped millions of Ethiopians embrace their culture through his stories.
  • The Magician: Tesfaye's role in Ethiopian entertainment extends to the illusion tricks he played in the TV programs.

He will always be remembered by millions of Ethiopians from different walks of life for the work he did. Learn more about Tesfaye from here!
